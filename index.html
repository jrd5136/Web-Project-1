It's All About Indian Music

Music has been always attended centrally among all Indians. Fundamentally, Indian Music is the history of some 2000 years of Indian people, Civilization and culture. The origin of Indian Music is religious and sound is called God namely "Nada- Brahma" in the Vedic hymns. Human consciousness can be raised to a degree through music which allows one to understand boundless aspect of universe. Earlier, All the music was sung in Sanskrit language only. In early 13th Century, Indian music subdivided in Hindustani Music which was centered in Hindi (North India) and Carnatic which was influenced by south India.
